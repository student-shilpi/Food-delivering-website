About FOODKART

Who We Are

FOODKART is a global restaurant discovery and food delivery platform founded in 2025. It helps users find the best places to eat around them and order food online from their favorite restaurants.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure nobody has a bad meal. We aim to make dining experiences more accessible, enjoyable, and affordable through technology.

What We Offer

  • Restaurant discovery
  • Online food ordering
  • Table reservations
  • Restaurant reviews and ratings
